About The Game

This a game made in 6 months as an assignment for the university.
I worked as junior level designer and junior Unity developer.
The game is a 3D top down shooter and adventure. Player will going through a massive dungeon combined of various smaller dungeon. All smaller dungeon are all random generated. Your goal is to find the boss room and defeat it.
